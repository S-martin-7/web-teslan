**Quick reminders:**
- Copy is Chilean Spanish, operational, sentence case, no emoji.
- Brand gradient is navy → blue → cyan (`#0A2A6B → #1B52C2 → #2BC4D9`); CTA is orange `#E87824`.
- Photography-first — do not generate illustrations or textured backgrounds.
- Use Lucide icons (CDN) until a bespoke set is provided.
